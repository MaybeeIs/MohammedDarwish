Mohammed Mustafa Darwish ABOUT ME WORKS CONTACT
Mohammed Mustafa Darwish, was born in 1950 in syria. he got a license in arabic literature from the university of damascus in 1975 He worked in al thawra al souria newspaper as an editor in its literature section. He is a member in the Arab writers union, he is also a member in the Syrian Arab Republic Union of Journalists, he is also a member in the federation of arab journalists. Currently living in West Virginia, Charleston, USA POET & JOURNALIST
